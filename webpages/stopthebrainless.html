Stop the Brainless

Description

Stop the Brainless is the first game I created within Unreal Engine, it was a for my final project of the year that would determine my grade. Stop the Brainless is a first-person, single-player, wave-shooter game set at a farm, the objective of the game is to protect the golden brain from the incoming wave of zombies.
